    About Me

    In the bustling heart of Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh, I am Tahmid Hasan Oni, a graduate student navigating the corridors of the prestigious Department of Mechanical Engineering, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). I obtained my B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from the same institution in 2023. Now, I am looking for PhD opportunities in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. I have research interests in Advanced Materials and Manufacturing. And would love to work with them in future. I believe that I am a very ambitious person. My future goal so far is to be in the industry, contribute as much as I can, and leave a legacy that catalyzes progress in my community and beyond.
    Apart from academics, I often actively volunteer and engage in several extra-curricular activities. I love to play guitar in my leisure time and and have a dream to set out for a world tour someday.
    Life, to me, is about exploring new paths and finding joy in simple things. As I make my way through the world, I embrace every opportunity to learn and grow along the way.
